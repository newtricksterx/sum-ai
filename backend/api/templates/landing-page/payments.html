FAQ

Pricing, answered.

Is ReadToRecall free?

Yes. The Free plan covers 10 summaries per month with 3 saved sessions — no credit card required. Standard and Pro unlock more summaries, larger inputs, and longer history.

What happens when I hit my monthly limit?

You can keep reading previously saved summaries, but new ones pause until the next billing cycle — or until you upgrade.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All paid plans bill monthly and can be cancelled at any time. You keep access until the end of the current cycle.

Which currencies do you support?

USD, CAD, and EUR — use the switcher above the cards to see pricing in your currency.

Do paid plans include flashcards and quizzes?

Yes — flashcards and quizzes are available on every plan. Paid tiers simply lift the summary, history, and input-length limits.
